About us

Welcome to the Creators Co-working Team Office, located on the iconic Chapel Street in Prahran. Common Spaces is meticulously designed for creative teams, offering everything you need to thrive. From our sound-treated podcast room and two versatile boardrooms to a media lounge equipped with an 85" screen and surround sound audio, we have it all. Enjoy a coffee at our stylish bar while taking in the best views Chapel Street has to offer. This space is perfect for teams of 10-25 who gather once a week, providing a complete and inspiring solution. Join us and elevate your collaborative efforts in a space designed for innovation and creativity.
